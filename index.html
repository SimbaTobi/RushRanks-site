How it works

1) Watch & upload
Browse hundreds of short videos, then upload your own Rush (≤ 100s) when you’re ready.
2) Play in your state
Your upload competes only in the state where you live. You can watch and like any state’s videos.
3) Win the day
Leaderboards lock at midnight (CT). Daily state champs and a national #1 are crowned.

Web share links for individual videos are coming in 2.0. For now, share inside the app or share the app links.

Why creators like it

Pure likes decide ranks
Every leaderboard is determined by likes only. It’s simple, transparent, and easy to rally your fans.
Boost your other socials
Winners can showcase and grow their other platforms—add your social links to your RushRanks profile and funnel the spotlight.
Fast cycles
Fresh chances every day. Miss today? Try again tomorrow.

Analytics

FAQ

When do leaderboards reset?
Every night at 11:59:59 PM Central Time (CT).
Who can use RushRanks?
People in the United States, age 13 or older.
How are ranks calculated?
Likes only. One person, one like per Rush.
