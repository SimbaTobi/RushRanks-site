FAQ

When do leaderboards reset?

Every night at 11:59:59 PM (local time). Winners post right after.

How are rankings calculated?

Unique likes + completion rate + views with light recency decay and anti‑cheat.

Is there a cost?

RushRanks is free. Ads may appear to support the platform.
