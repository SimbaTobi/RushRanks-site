Last updated: 2025-10-12

RushRanks is a product of Sandook LLC (“we”, “us”). This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Account: email address and state selection.
  • Content: videos you upload (“Rushes”), likes, comments, and related metadata.
  • Usage & device: app events, crash logs, IP‑derived region, device identifiers.
  • Support: messages you send to our support email.

How we use information

  • Operate and improve RushRanks, including moderation and fraud prevention.
  • Run daily leaderboards and publish winners to the Rushchive.
  • Provide analytics, notifications, and core engagement features.
  • Show ads and measure their performance, if enabled.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

Sharing

We share data with service providers (hosting, delivery, moderation, analytics, ads). We may share to comply with law or protect rights.

Children

RushRanks is for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your choices

  • Email support@rushranks.com to access or delete your account.
  • Use device/OS settings to limit ad tracking.
  • Opt out of non‑essential marketing emails via the link in those messages.

Data retention

We retain account data while your account is active. Content and logs may be retained for safety, security, and product integrity.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

International

We may process and store information in the United States and other countries.
